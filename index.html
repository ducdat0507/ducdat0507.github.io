  • flagships

    The idle game that gives you a whopping 1 followed by 12 zeros of free gacha cards for you to draw, at least if you can draw all of them.

    Inspired by and parodies the trend of advertisements for gacha games that promises numbers of draws as if it's a selling point. Of course, with the power of being able to make games myself and a crave for big numbers in games, I felt the need to inflate the amount even harder than what was what, four thousand?

    It's fairly mobile-friendly and should be completable in about 2~3 days of active idling.
